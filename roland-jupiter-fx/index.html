Welcome to Modularsamples, a public-domain sample library.

Each library is playable from with your browser, enter notes into the sequencer below or use a QWERTY keyboard using A - @ for white keys, W - P for black and Z & X to transpose.  Additionally MIDI input is enabled for Chrome and Opera users, just plugin a device and play!
